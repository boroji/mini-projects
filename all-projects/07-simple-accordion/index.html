About us

We created Boroji in 2003 to serve as a digital content distribution channel, before app stores existed. It's since grown and evolved into a platform for thousands of creators and publishers to deliver content and establish direct relationships with their customers. The Boroji Community enables millions of players to do likewise, sharing entertainment and ideas, and making friends.

Partnership

We make consumer electronic devices that improve exercise on the PC. Things like the Boroji Controller and the Boroji Link. We invented the Boroji technologies that power the VR, an interactive VR system brought to market in partnership with Boroji Inc. Just as in software development, we conduct playtests and share prototypes with our partners and players.
